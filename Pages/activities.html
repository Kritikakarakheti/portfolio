Patan Mental Hospital Report


Few information from the psychologist during my visit for the project.

I got the chance to interact with psychologists face-to-face and ask the questions I was interested in during my work as a research associate for the 1166 National Helpline for Suicide Prevention project conducted by WHO.  The meeting lasted for about 2.5 hours. I made a list of queries that piqued my interest. I took note of all his responses in addition to my questions so that I might write a report later. Global Landscape Forum (GLF) Climate 2022


I am so glad that I volunteered as a rapporteur for the Global Landscapes Forum (GLF) Climate 2022 Hybrid Conference: Frontiers of Change. It was indeed a great learning experience.
I mostly focused on two sessions naming,

1. Unlocking solutions to finance ecosystem restoration
“This session discussed the challenges and opportunities for increasing restoration finance, building on the work of the UN Decade Finance Task Force (FTF). The session also provided a space to exchange promising solutions for financing, projects and business models that serve as a blueprint for replicable and scalable approaches.”

2. Early Warning- Early Action Systems for transboundary threats
“This session explored how governments and other stakeholders can better use artificial intelligence and other remotely-sensed early warning data to effectively tackle transboundary climate hazards, including droughts, floods, crop pests, and pathogen outbreaks.”

National Children Theatre Festival


I was chosen to appear in the 3rd National Children's Theatre Festival and represent my school because I have always had a natural talent for acting and a desire to bring characters to life on stage or in films. Faaliyeka Heera, which means "Thrown Diamonds," is the name of our act. Many abortions took place at that time only because the unborn child was a girl. We conveyed the significance of gender equality and the value of women in society via our performance.
