ABOUT ME

My name is Kritika Karakheti.


I have a strong desire, passion, and zeal for understanding the wonders of the human brain. I’ve always been fascinated by the interesting connection between the emotions that constantly swirl about in our heads, behavioral control, various mood swings, and their mechanism. I became interested in human psychology which later turned into a strong obsession. I love to discuss psychological ideas and how they may be applied to explain human behavior. I study and research several parts of the brain that intrigues me. During one of my studies, I was extremely inspired and captivated by the concept of the placebo effect, leading me to delve more into it and produce a paper.


I am more interested in computational neuroscience, molecular biology, cognitive and behavioral neuroscience, neuroimaging, and neuroplasticity. If any of these topics are also of interest to you, then I would be more than happy to connect.


I am also a writer. I often use writing as a tool to vent out my emotions about the incidents that play a certain role in my life. Writing makes me feel more lively. It is a means for me to find comfort and articulate my thoughts about feelings, nature, and knowledge as well as look back on life experiences. I write both fiction and non-fiction.


Besides these, I adore going to the library since I enjoy the vibe of me being surrounded by numerous books. I love traveling, hiking, meeting new people, listening to others’ experiences and stories, and have had a divine interest in music since childhood. I also enjoy the aura of meditation.


Being someone who is truly concerned about the importance of mental health, I have also done a few projects regarding it. Click here to directly join me in my Portfolio section to see more. And if you are also interested in these fields and have any project ideas then get in touch! Let’s connect!
